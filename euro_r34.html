4 · Defense vs Position (DvP) & Matchup

What it measures: How many fantasy points the opposing team allows on average to each position (Guard / Forward / Center). A team with a high DvP for Guards = Guards score a lot against them = EASY matchup for your Guard.

Data source: InCrowd Sports API (official EuroLeague data feed). All completed games this season, per-player stats extracted, approx FPT computed as: pts + reb + ast×1.5 + stl×2 + blk×2 − tov

Multiplier: Each team's DvP is divided by the league average for that position. multiplier = team_DvP / league_avg_DvP. A multiplier of 1.10 means opponents score 10% above average against that team. This multiplier is applied to ExpFPT to get AdjFPT.

🟢 EASY — best 25% of matchups 🟡 FAVORABLE — top 26–50% 🟠 TOUGH — bottom 26–50% 🔴 HARD — worst 25%

6 · Team Builder

Scoring formula (mirrors Fantaking):

Score = Captain×2 + Starter₂ + … + Starter₆ + Bench₁×0.5 + … + Bench₄×0.5

All scoring uses AdjFPT (matchup-adjusted) when available, otherwise ExpFPT. The captain = player with highest AdjFPT (scores 2× in Fantaking).

ParameterRule
EligibilityGP ≥ 5, played within Max Inactive Rounds, Rec ≠ N/A, not INJURED
Starters (6)Picked greedily by highest AdjFPT within starter budget
Bench (4)Picked by best AdjFPT/CR efficiency (bench scores 0.5×, so cost matters more)
3 Teams🥇 Best Team → 🥈 Alt A (excludes top 2 from T1) → 🥉 Alt B (excludes top 4). Gives meaningful alternatives.
RETURNING penalty−10% (absent ≤2 rounds) · −18% (3–4 rounds) · −30% (5+ rounds)
