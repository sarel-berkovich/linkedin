4 ¬∑ Defense vs Position (DvP) & Matchup

What it measures: How many fantasy points the opposing team allows on average to each position (Guard / Forward / Center). A team with a high DvP for Guards = Guards score a lot against them = EASY matchup for your Guard.

Data source: InCrowd Sports API (official EuroLeague data feed). All completed games this season, per-player stats extracted, approx FPT computed as: pts + reb + ast√ó1.5 + stl√ó2 + blk√ó2 ‚àí tov

Multiplier: Each team's DvP is divided by the league average for that position. multiplier = team_DvP / league_avg_DvP. A multiplier of 1.10 means opponents score 10% above average against that team. This multiplier is applied to ExpFPT to get AdjFPT.
